Waterloo Start Team Lead

What is Waterloo Start?
Waterloo Start is a first year orientation and onboarding system. We follow the Five Ways to Well Being developed the New Economic Foundation from evidence gathered in the UK government's Foresight Project on Mental CApital and Wellbeing. The five ways to improve our wellbeing include connecting to other people, being active, taking notie, learning, and giving. To find out more please visit https://waterloostart.github.io/.



Tasks as the team lead

  • Plan schedule and arrange group meetings
  • Plan icebreakers to encourage a friendly work environment where people feel comfortable with eachother
  • Discuss and set goals with team
  • Create an action plan to achieve goals
  • Monitor and track progress
  • Make any necessarily adjustments to plan
  • Ensure everyone on the team understands their role in the team and feels comfortable to freely communicate their ideas and opinions
  • Suggest improvements and encourage other members to be self-starters and innovative
  • Problem solving
  • reporting to professor about our team's progress and asking for advice on where to improve
  • Ensure all documents are well organized and easily accessible
  • Extra: Ideating, content writing and programming in HTML and CSS
